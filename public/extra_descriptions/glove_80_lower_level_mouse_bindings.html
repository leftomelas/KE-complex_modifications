Mouse Emulation for Glove 80 Lower Layer

This rule provides full mouse control using the Glove 80 keyboard's lower layer, designed for Mac Mouse Keys style navigation. Movement controls are on the right hand (numpad), while modifiers and click controls are on the left hand.

Note: All commands assume you are in the Glove 80's lower layer. By default, access the Glove 80's lower layer by holding the Layer key.

Movement (Right Hand - Numpad):

  • Keypad 8: Move mouse up
  • Keypad 2: Move mouse down
  • Keypad 4: Move mouse left
  • Keypad 6: Move mouse right
  • Keypad 7: Move mouse diagonally up-left
  • Keypad 9: Move mouse diagonally up-right
  • Keypad 1: Move mouse diagonally down-left
  • Keypad 3: Move mouse diagonally down-right

Click Controls:

  • Keypad 5: Left click (right hand)
  • F11: Left click (left hand)
  • F12: Right click (left hand)

Note: The left hand left click (F11) is particularly useful for click-and-hold operations, as it frees up your right hand to continue controlling mouse movement with the numpad.

Modifiers (Left Hand):

  • End (hold): Scroll mode - hold End and use numpad keys to scroll instead of move
  • Page Up: Fast mode (2.0x speed)
  • Page Down: Slow mode (0.3x speed)

Scroll Mode:

Hold the End key and use the numpad keys to scroll:

  • End + Keypad 8: Scroll up
  • End + Keypad 2: Scroll down
  • End + Keypad 4: Scroll left
  • End + Keypad 6: Scroll right
  • End + Keypad 7: Scroll diagonally up-left
  • End + Keypad 9: Scroll diagonally up-right
  • End + Keypad 1: Scroll diagonally down-left
  • End + Keypad 3: Scroll diagonally down-right

Example Usage:

  1. Press Page Up to activate fast mode
  2. Use Keypad 8/2/4/6 to move the mouse quickly
  3. Press F11 to left click
  4. Hold End and press Keypad 2 to scroll down
  5. Press Page Down to activate slow mode for precise control

Designed specifically for the Glove 80 split ergonomic keyboard's lower layer layout without a need to update the firmware.
